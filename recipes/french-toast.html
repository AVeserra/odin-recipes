French Toast

Recipe Description

I will make this whenever my girlfriend is not feeling well :)

Ingredients:

  • Milk - 1/4 Cup
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 Teaspoon
  • Cinnamon - 1/2 Teaspoon
  • Egg - 1
  • Bread Slice - 4

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the egg, cinnamon, and vanilla extract in a shallow dish
  2. Stir in milk
  3. Soak both sides of the bread slice in mixture
  4. In a greased pan at medium heat, place the bread and let cook for 3 minutes on each side
