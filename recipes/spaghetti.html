Spaghetti

Recipe Description

Simple and classic recipe for a fast dinner

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef - 1lb.
  • Spaghetti Noodles - 1 Box
  • Tomato Sauce - 1 Jar

Instructions:

  1. Boil a pot of water and put the noodles in for 12 minutes, stirring occasionally
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Once the beef is browned, pour the sauce into the pan of beef and mix well
