Taco

Recipe Description

A nice comfort food that's a quick cook

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef - 1lb.
  • Tortillas - 10
  • Spices - 4 Tablespoons
  • Water - 1/2 Cup

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Once the beef is browned, add the spices and the water
  3. Stir and let simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Warm tortillas
